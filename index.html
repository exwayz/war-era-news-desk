About News Desk

Powered by:
gateway.warerastats.io
api2.warera.io
api5.warera.io

The War Era News Desk was created to support players who take on the role of journalists, providing a centralized workspace to monitor, organize, and collect information from every significant event unfolding across the world of War Era.

Its primary purpose is to enhance the journalistic experience by making information easier to discover and analyze while preserving the core principles of journalism: credibility, transparency, and accuracy.

The project is developed as Free and Open Source Software (FOSS), allowing anyone to inspect the source code, contribute to its development, or adapt it for their own needs. The source repository is available at:

https://github.com/exwayz/war-era-news-desk

From its very beginning, War Era News Desk has embraced the KISS (Keep It Simple, Stupid) philosophy. Every feature is designed with practicality in mind, favoring clarity, efficiency, and reliability over unnecessary visual complexity. The goal is not to build the most elaborate interface, but to create a dependable newsroom tool that helps journalists focus on what matters most: the story.
