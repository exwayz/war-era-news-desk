# Product

## Register

product

## Users

War Era journalists and content creators who need a real-time newsroom dashboard. They're producing content about the game — articles, reports, analysis — and need live data feeds, entity resolution, and a tool that feels like a professional news desk rather than a game overlay.

## Product Purpose

Aggregate War Era's real-time event stream, battle intelligence, market economics, and community data into a single, fast, flat dashboard. Success means a journalist can open one page and get everything they need to report on the current state of the game world.

## Brand Personality

Sharp, editorial, utilitarian. Newspaper-industrial — no-nonsense, data-forward, flat. Communicates density and authority like a wire service terminal, not polish and delight like a consumer app.

## Anti-references

- No SaaS clichés: no dribbble-style dashboards, heavy shadows, gradients, rounded mega-cards, or hero-metric templates
- No game-UI aesthetics: this is a tool, not part of War Era itself — avoid themed skins, fantasy flourishes, decorative elements
- Not a "startup landing page" — no testimonials, feature grids, or signup funnels

## Design Principles

- **Information density first** — every pixel should carry signal; nothing is decorative
- **Flat and uncompromising** — no border-radius, no backdrop-filter, no box-shadow; border lines and background tints do the separating
- **Newspaper tiling** — panels abut with no gap, like a broadsheet layout or a wire service terminal
- **Typographic hierarchy** — Playfair Display for headings, Literata for body, JetBrains Mono for data; contrast drives hierarchy, not decorative effects
- **Editorial tone** — the UI reads like a publication, not an app; labels are clear, voice is neutral, data speaks first

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA contrast minimums on all text
- Light and dark themes
- Prefers-reduced-motion respected (no essential content gated on animation)
